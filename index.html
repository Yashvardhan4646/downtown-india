Server Guidelines

Simple rules to keep our community awesome for everyone

No Spam or Self-Promotion

Keep conversations meaningful. No excessive spam, ads, or unsolicited promotions.

Use Appropriate Channels

Post content in the correct channels. Gaming in gaming channels, memes in meme channels, etc.

Follow staff instructions. They’re here to keep the server safe and enjoyable for everyone.
